Chapter 38: The Price of a Soul

第三十八章：灵魂的标价

"Let's skip the foreplay."
She produced a cigarette instead of a weapon.

“跳过前戏吧。”
她掏出的不是枪，而是一支烟。

"The drive for the shares.
Ten percent. Non-negotiable."

“U盘换股份。
10%。不议价。”

"You are in no position to negotiate,"
Mishima sneered.

“你没有谈判资格。”

Chen Mo stepped out,
raising a detonator.

陈默走出车门，
举起引爆器。

"The car is rigged with C4,"
he lied calmly.

“车上有C4。”
他平静地撒谎。

Mishima laughed—
sharp, delighted.

三岛大笑，
锋利而兴奋。

A briefcase emerged from the shadows.

阴影中递出金属手提箱。

"Five percent.
Final offer."

“5%。
最终报价。”

"Deal."

“成交。”

The USB flew through the air—

U盘被抛向空中——

BANG!

砰！

Shattered plastic rained down like glitter.

塑料碎片如闪光雨坠落。
