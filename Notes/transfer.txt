Chapter 58: The Predators at the Table

第五十八章：餐桌上的掠食者

"L'Osier." One of the most expensive French restaurants in Ginza.

“L'Osier”。银座最昂贵的法餐厅之一。

When "Mr. and Mrs. Tanaka" walked in, the entire dining room went silent.

当“田中夫妇”走进去时，整个餐厅都安静了。

Miyetsuko wore large sunglasses and a wide-brimmed hat, channeling a 1950s movie star vibe. But no disguise could hide that body. The way the red silk clung to her hips, the way her chest swayed with a heavy, hypnotic rhythm as she walked—it was a gravitational force.

美悦子戴着大墨镜和宽檐帽，散发着50年代电影明星的气质。但没有任何伪装能通过藏住那具身体。红色丝绸紧贴她臀部的方式，她走路时胸部那沉重、催眠般的摇晃节奏——那是地心引力般的吸引力。

They were seated at the best table.

他们被安排在最好的桌子。

"Wagyu filet. Rare. And a bottle of Romanée-Conti 1990," Miyetsuko ordered without looking at the menu.

“和牛菲力。三分熟。再来一瓶1990年的罗曼尼·康帝，”美悦子看都没看菜单就点了。

The sommelier almost dropped his wine list. That bottle cost more than a car.

侍酒师差点掉了酒单。那瓶酒比一辆车还贵。

"To freedom," Chen Mo raised his glass.

“敬自由，”陈默举杯。

"To the idiot paying for this," Miyetsuko clinked her glass against his.

“敬那个为此买单的蠢货，”美悦子和他碰杯。

They ate like wolves in human skin. Miyetsuko cut the meat, the red juice flowing onto the white porcelain plate. She put a piece in her mouth, chewing slowly, her red lips shiny with grease and wine.

他们像披着人皮的狼一样进食。美悦子切开肉，红色的肉汁流在白瓷盘上。她放了一块在嘴里，慢慢咀嚼，红唇上闪着油光和酒渍。

Under the table, covered by the long tablecloth, Chen Mo's hand rested on her knee.

在桌子底下，长长的桌布掩护下，陈默的手放在她的膝盖上。

He slid his hand up, through the high slit of her dress.

他的手向上滑，穿过礼服的高开叉。

"Chen..." she stopped chewing, her fork hovering in mid-air.

“陈……”她停止了咀嚼，叉子悬在半空。

His fingers touched her bare inner thigh. The skin was hot and smooth. He went higher, brushing against the lace of the new panties she had bought.

他的手指触到了她裸露的大腿内侧。皮肤滚烫光滑。他继续向上，擦过她刚买的新内裤的蕾丝边。

"The waiter is watching," she whispered, taking a sip of wine to hide her flush.

“服务员在看，”她低声说，喝了一口酒来掩饰脸红。

"Let him watch," Chen Mo's fingers slipped under the lace. "You said you liked danger."

“让他看，”陈默的手指滑进了蕾丝下面。“你说过你喜欢危险。”

Miyetsuko gripped the edge of the table. Her eyes behind the sunglasses widened. In the middle of a Michelin 3-star restaurant, surrounded by Tokyo's elite, she was being fingered by her lover. The suppression of her moan mixed with the taste of the expensive wine was intoxicating.

美悦子抓住了桌沿。墨镜后的眼睛瞪大了。在米其林三星餐厅的中央，被东京的精英们包围着，她正在被她的情人爱抚。压抑的呻吟混合着昂贵红酒的味道，令人陶醉。
