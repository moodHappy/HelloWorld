Dave stumbled out of the bar, convinced the world had gone wonderfully crooked. Trees danced the tango, and fireflies were tiny fairies with lanterns. He tried to high-five a lamppost, which he mistook for a very tall, very bright man. "You're illuminating, friend!" Dave slurred.

His phone rang, displaying three versions of his wife. "Which one should I answer?" he wondered aloud. When he finally reached home, he hugged the refrigerator, whispering, "You're the coolest person I know."

The next morning, Dave's world was painfully straight again, save for the Post-it note on his forehead: "Next time, drink water between adventures."
