Before the Wig

假发之前

1. The Call

那通电话

The call came while she was folding clean laundry on the bed.

电话来的时候，她正把洗好的衣服叠在床上。

A sock slipped from her hand and stayed on the floor.

一只袜子从她手里滑落，留在地上。

The voice on the line was calm and practiced.

电话那头的声音很冷静，也很熟练。

They used short sentences and did not repeat her name.

对方用很短的句子，没有再叫她的名字。

When the call ended, the room felt smaller.

电话挂断后，房间变小了。

2. The Mirror

镜子

She stood in front of the bathroom mirror for a long time.

她在浴室镜子前站了很久。

Her hair reached her shoulders and curled inward at the ends.

头发到肩膀，发尾微微内扣。

She had trimmed it herself many times before.

她以前也自己修剪过很多次。

This time, she did not look at the length.

这一次，她没有看长度。

She looked at the roots, close to the scalp.

她盯着靠近头皮的发根。

3. The Decision

决定

The scissors were under the sink, wrapped in a thin towel.

剪刀在水池下面，用一条薄毛巾包着。

She remembered buying them with her mother years ago.

她记得很多年前和母亲一起买的。

The metal felt cold in her palm.

金属在手心里很凉。

She told herself she would just make it shorter.

她对自己说，只是剪短一点。

She did not say it out loud.

她没有说出口。

4. Cutting

剪下去

The first cut was uneven and loud.

第一刀不整齐，也很响。

Hair slid down her neck and stuck to her skin.

头发滑过脖子，贴在皮肤上。

She kept going because stopping felt worse.

她继续剪下去，因为停下来更难受。

The sink began to clog with dark strands.

水池里慢慢堆满了黑色的发丝。

Her arm started to ache.

她的手臂开始发酸。

5. After

之后

When she finished, the mirror showed a stranger who looked alert.

剪完后，镜子里的人很陌生，却显得警觉。

Her ears were fully visible.

耳朵完全露了出来。

She rinsed the sink and wiped the floor.

她把水池冲干净，又擦了地。

The trash bag felt heavier than it should.

垃圾袋比想象中要重。

She tied it and left it by the door.

她把袋子系好，放在门边。

6. The Quiet

安静

That night, she slept without tying her hair back.

那天晚上，她睡觉时没有再把头发扎起来。

Her neck felt cool against the pillow.

脖子贴着枕头，有点凉。

She woke once and reached for hair that was no longer there.

她醒过一次，伸手去摸已经不存在的头发。

Her hand stopped in the air.

手停在半空中。

She lay still until morning.

她一动不动地躺到天亮。
