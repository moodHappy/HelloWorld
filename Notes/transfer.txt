In the tapestry of life, two threads intertwined: love and friendship. Li and Wei were the best of friends, their bond unbreakable. Until one day, a gentle breeze introduced a third thread - romance. Li's heart fluttered for Wei, but fear of losing the friendship held them back. However, love's pull was stronger. With courage, Li confessed, and to their surprise, Wei felt the same. Love and friendship didn't war, but instead, wove together, creating a stronger, more beautiful bond that would last a lifetime. In their story, "Ai Qing/Yu Qing," love and friendship danced harmoniously.
