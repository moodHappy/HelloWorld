As Detective Li investigated the mysterious murder, he discovered a cryptic message at the crime scene. The victim, a renowned professor, had been researching an ancient novel. Li noticed a peculiar symbol etched into the wall, matching one in the professor's book. Unraveling the mystery, Li deduced that the symbol indicated a hidden chapter. Upon finding the chapter, he uncovered a shocking truth: the professor had been blackmailing a former student, who had committed the crime. Li's sharp reasoning cracked the case, revealing the dark secrets behind the professor's research. Justice was finally served.
