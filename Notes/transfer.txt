The fluorescent lights hummed as Sarah stared at her computer screen. Monday emails flooded her inbox. Her coworker Mike appeared, coffee mug in hand.

"Big presentation today," he said nervously.

Sarah nodded, offering an encouraging smile. They had worked weeks preparing. As the conference room filled with executives, Sarah took a deep breath.

The slides flowed perfectly. When she finished, the room erupted in applause. Mike gave her a thumbs-up. Back at her desk, Sarah finally relaxed. Another challenge met in the endless cycle of office life.
