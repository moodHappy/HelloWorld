Chapter 15: The Kiss of Death

第十五章：死亡之吻

"Now... turn around..." Rodriguez ordered. "I want to take you from behind. I want to see your face in the mirror while I break you."

“现在……转过去……”罗德里格斯命令道。“我要从后面干你。我要一边干烂你，一边在镜子里看你的脸。”

Chen Mo heard the bedsprings creak.

陈默听到床垫弹簧的嘎吱声。

"Unlock the tablet first," Miyetsuko's voice was sultry, dripping with fake desire. "Record us. I want to watch it later."

“先解锁平板，”美悦子的声音充满媚态，滴着伪装的欲望。“把我们录下来。我想待会儿看。”

"You freak... I like it."

“你这个变态……我喜欢。”

Beep. Beep.

滴。滴。

"Face ID recognized." The automated voice of the tablet.

“面部ID已识别。”平板电脑的自动语音。

"Now..." Rodriguez grunted, positioning himself behind her.

“现在……”罗德里格斯哼了一声，在她身后摆好姿势。

Chen Mo heard the slap of flesh. Rodriguez was spanking her. Hard.

陈默听到了肉体拍打声。罗德里格斯在打她的屁股。很重。

"Come on, big boy," Miyetsuko whispered. "Give it to me."

“来吧，大块头，”美悦子低语。“给我。”

And then, a different sound.

然后，是一个不同的声音。

Not a moan. But a sharp, wet shuck sound. Like a knife slicing through a melon.

不是呻吟。而是一声尖锐、湿润的噗嗤声。像刀切开西瓜。

"Gurgle... ck... ck..."

“咯咯……咳……咳……”

Rodriguez’s roar turned into a wet gurgle.

罗德里格斯的吼叫变成了湿润的咯咯声。

"Shh... quiet, pig." Miyetsuko's voice was cold as ice.

“嘘……安静，猪。”美悦子的声音冷得像冰。

Chen Mo kicked the door open.

陈默踢开了门。

The scene inside was a masterpiece of gore.

里面的场景是一幅血腥的杰作。

Miyetsuko was kneeling on the bed, naked, her back to the door. Rodriguez was behind her, slumped over her back.

美悦子跪在床上，赤裸着，背对着门。罗德里格斯在她身后，瘫倒在她背上。

A gold hairpin—six inches long and sharp as a needle—was buried in his eye socket, driven straight into the brain.

一根金发簪——六英寸长，像针一样锋利——插在他的眼眶里，直入大脑。

His blood was pumping out, spraying over Miyetsuko’s white back, running down her spine, mixing with his sweat and her perfume. It dyed her skin a bright, violent crimson.

他的血正在泵出，喷溅在美悦子雪白的背上，顺着她的脊椎流下，混合着他的汗水和她的香水。把她的皮肤染成了鲜艳、暴力的深红。

She held the unlocked tablet in one hand. With the other hand, she pushed the twitching corpse off her.

她一只手拿着解锁的平板。另一只手把还在抽搐的尸体推开。
