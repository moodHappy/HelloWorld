In a bustling downtown office, Sarah and Tom worked side by side in a cramped cubicle. Their shared love for Excel spreadsheets soon blossomed into a secret office romance. Amidst late-night deadlines and coffee breaks, they shared stolen glances and whispered conversations. Their love grew stronger, despite the office's strict anti-fraternization policy. One day, Tom mustered the courage to propose an idea to Sarah: they could leave their jobs and start their own business together. With a leap of faith, they stepped into the unknown, and their love thrived in the freedom of their new venture.
