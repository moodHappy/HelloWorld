A mass shooting took place in Stockton, California, during a family gathering. Four people were killed, and ten others were hurt. Police believe the shooting was targeted, not random, and some of the victims were children. The situation shocked the local community, and the police are now looking for information to find the attackers. Many people hope the city will become safer soon.[1]
