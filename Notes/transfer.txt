The old library's silence was broken when Sarah found a handwritten note tucked in a dusty book. "They're watching," it read. She glanced around, but saw only the usual patrons and Mrs. Higgins, the elderly librarian who'd worked there for decades.

As she turned to leave, Sarah noticed Mrs. Higgins watching her with an unnerving intensity. The book in Sarah's bag suddenly felt heavy. When she looked back, the librarian was gone. On the circulation desk lay another note: "Some books should never be checked out." Sarah clutched the book tighter. What had she discovered?
