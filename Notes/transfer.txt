**Local Shooting Leaves One Dead**

Police are investigating a fatal shooting that occurred downtown yesterday evening. According to witnesses, an unidentified assailant fired multiple shots at approximately 6:30 PM near the Central Market, striking a 42-year-old male victim who later died at City Hospital.

"The suspect fled the scene in a dark-colored vehicle," stated Police Chief Johnson. "We are asking anyone with information to come forward."

Authorities have not yet determined a motive for the attack. This marks the third shooting incident in the area this month. The victim's name is being withheld pending family notification. An ongoing investigation continues.
