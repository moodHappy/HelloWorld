Chapter 41: Bullet Ballet in the Dark

第四十一章：黑暗中的子弹芭蕾

"Three!"
“三！”

Chen Mo and Miyetsuko rolled out from beneath the car at the same instant.

陈默与美悦子同时从车底翻滚而出。

The warehouse had become a chaotic disco of death.
Muzzle flashes strobed through darkness—
revealing fragments of violence in frozen light.

仓库化作死亡的迪斯科。
枪口焰在黑暗中频闪——
把暴力切成一帧帧定格。

A mercenary falling.
Blood mist bursting from the rafters.
Kurosaki’s cane crushing a man’s face.

雇佣兵倒下。
房梁血雾炸开。
黑崎的手杖砸碎面孔。

They sprinted toward the rear exit.

他们向后门狂奔。

Miyetsuko led the way.
The racing leather resisted every stride,
creaking against motion.

美悦子冲在前方。
赛车皮衣抗拒着每一次迈步，
摩擦声清晰可闻。

"Cover me!"

“掩护我！”

Chen Mo turned and emptied his magazine into the pursuing shadows.
One mercenary collapsed, clutching his knee.

陈默回身清空弹夹。
一名雇佣兵捂膝倒地。

They burst through the rear door—
into cold, salty night air.

他们撞开后门——
冲入咸湿夜风。
