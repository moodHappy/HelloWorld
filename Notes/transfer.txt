Chapter 43: Wet Leather and Sea Spray

第四十三章：湿皮衣与海浪

At sixty knots,
the wind felt solid as a wall.

六十节航速下，
风如实体之墙。

They flew across the waves.
Yokohama’s lights receded—
melting into scattered jewels.

他们在浪尖飞驰。
横滨灯火后退——
化作远方碎钻。

She dragged herself into the passenger seat,
soaked in seawater.

她爬进副驾驶，
被海水彻底浸透。

Salt tightened the leather like black glass.
Hair clung to her face.

盐水让皮革紧贴如黑色镜面。
湿发贴在脸颊。

She began to laugh—
sharp, hysterical, unstoppable.

她忽然大笑——
尖锐、失控、停不下来。

"We did it…"

“我们做到了……”

"Don’t celebrate yet."
Chen Mo pointed at the radar.

“别高兴太早。”
陈默指向雷达屏。

Two red dots closing fast.

两个红点高速逼近。

Searchlights sweeping the sea behind them.

探照灯在后方海面扫射。

"Then let’s make this interesting."

“那就让事情更有趣一点。”

She climbed across the console,
reaching into the glove compartment.

她越过中控，
伸手探入手套箱。

A flare gun.
A heavy red canister.

信号枪。
沉重的红色罐体。

"Nitrous," she grinned.

“笑气。”她咧嘴一笑。

"Hold on tight…
I’m going to blow their minds."

“抓紧了……
我要让他们永生难忘。”
