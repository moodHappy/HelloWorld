In the year 2154, a new technology emerged that allowed people to upload their memories into a virtual world. At first, it was a novelty, but soon people became addicted to reliving their happiest moments. The government encouraged its use, claiming it would increase productivity and happiness. But as people spent more time in the virtual world, they began to lose touch with reality. They forgot about their problems and their relationships suffered. One day, a young woman named Maya realized that her virtual world was not as perfect as she thought. She discovered a glitch that revealed the dark truth: the government was using the technology to control people's minds. Maya tried to escape, but it was too late. She was trapped in the virtual world, forced to relive the same happy memories forever.
