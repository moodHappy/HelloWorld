Title: Mysterious Klimt Painting Sold for $236m

A painting by Gustav Klimt called "Portrait of Elisabeth Lederer" has been sold for $236 million. This makes it the most expensive modern art piece ever sold at an auction. The painting is special because it is a beautiful portrait of a 20-year-old woman from a rich family in Vienna. The painting has many hidden symbols and meanings, including signs from East Asian art and tiny medical images. 

The painting was created during World War I and shows the changing times and culture. The woman in the painting, Elisabeth Lederer, had a difficult life during World War II because she was Jewish. She even said that Klimt, the artist, was her father to protect herself from the Nazis. 

The painting is very valuable not just because of its beauty, but also because of its interesting history and the secrets it holds. It is now considered one of the most important artworks in the world, and people are excited to learn more about its story and the artist who created it.
