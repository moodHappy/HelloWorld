Title: Australia's Social Media Ban for Under 16s
* The Australian government has introduced a ban on social media for people under 16 years old.
* The ban aims to protect young people from online harm and promote a healthier online environment.
* Some popular social media apps, such as Snapchat and TikTok, are affected by the ban.
* Alternative social media apps, like Lemon8 and Yope, are gaining popularity as some teens try to avoid the ban.
* Teenagers and experts have mixed opinions about the ban, with some supporting it and others thinking it's not an effective solution.
* The government says the ban will help kids have a safer childhood and give parents more peace of mind.
* The social media companies will have to verify users' ages and comply with the ban.
* Australia is the first country to introduce such a ban, and other countries may follow its example.
* Some teenagers think the ban is a good idea, but others are finding ways to get around it by creating new accounts or using different apps.
* The government will monitor the effectiveness of the ban and make adjustments as needed.
